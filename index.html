3.2 Google Gemini AI (Educational Assistant)

Board Vibes uses Google Gemini Flash 2.0 to provide AI-powered tutoring. When using AI features:

  • Your questions are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing
  • Conversations are NOT stored on our servers
  • Google may process queries according to their API Terms
  • No personal information is included in AI requests
  • Daily usage is limited (no personal account required)

Learn more: Google Gemini API Terms

Privacy Protection: AI conversations are ephemeral and not tied to your identity. We do not create user profiles or track individual usage patterns.

3.3 Data NOT Collected by AI Features

Our AI features do NOT collect or store:

  • Your name or identity
  • Your specific answers to Daily Challenge questions
  • Chat conversation history
  • Individual usage patterns or profiles
  • Academic performance data linked to you

2.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored ONLY on your device and never leaves your phone:

  • Selected board, class, and medium preferences
  • Downloaded textbook PDFs
  • Quiz history and results
  • Bookmarks and reading progress
  • Study streak and statistics
  • Daily AI Challenge progress (XP, completed questions)
  • AI question usage count (resets daily, stored locally)
  • App settings (dark mode, notifications, etc.)

5. App Permissions

Board Vibes requests the following permissions:

5.1 Required Permissions

  • Internet Access: To download textbook PDFs, use AI features, and display ads
  • Storage Access: To save downloaded PDFs locally on your device

5.2 Optional Permissions

  • Notifications: To send study reminders (can be disabled in settings)

6. Children's Privacy

Board Vibes is designed for students aged 13 and above. We comply with children's privacy laws:

  • No personal information is collected from any user
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • Age-appropriate advertising only (Google AdMob compliant)
  • No social media integration
